We are proud to announce that the **Center of Ancient History and Culture, Jain (Deemed-to-be University)** has been awarded the prestigious **ISO 21001:2018 Certificate** for the period of 2025-2028. This news was officially received on April 24, 2026.

The ISO 21001:2018 is an international standard that provides a common management tool for organizations providing educational products and services, ensuring they are capable of meeting the needs and requirements of learners and other beneficiaries.

This recognition highlights the university's commitment to maintaining a high-quality educational management system, demonstrating its ability to support the acquisition and development of competence through teaching, learning, or research. The Center for Ancient History and Culture (CAHC) celebrates this institutional milestone, which reinforces our continued pursuit of academic excellence.
  </div>
  <div class="lang-content" lang="sa" markdown="1">
वयम् अत्यन्तं गौरवेण उद्घोषयामः यत् जैनविश्वविद्यालयस्य **पुरातनेतिहास-संस्कृति-केन्द्राय (CAHC)** २०२५-२०२८ कालखण्डाय प्रतिष्ठितं **ISO 21001:2018** प्रमाणीकरणपत्रं दत्तम् अस्ति। इयं वार्ता २४ अप्रैल, २०२६ दिनाङ्के अधिकृतरूपेण प्राप्ता।

ISO 21001:2018 इति अन्तर्राष्ट्रियं मानकमस्ति यत् शैक्षणिकसेवासंस्थाभ्यः प्रबन्धनसाधनं प्रददाति, येन शिक्षार्थीनां लाभार्थिनाञ्च आवश्यकतानां पूरणं सुनिश्चितं भवति।

इयं मान्यता विश्वविद्यालयस्य गुणवत्तापूर्णशैक्षणिकप्रबन्धने दृढसङ्कल्पं दर्शयति, यत् शिक्षण-अध्ययन-अनुसन्धानद्वारा योग्यताविकासं समर्थयति। पुरातनेतिहास-संस्कृति-केन्द्रं (CAHC) एतां गौरवपूर्णां संस्थात्मकावृत्तिं आचरति, यदस्माकं शैक्षणिकगुणवत्तायाः मार्गं सुदृढं करोति।
