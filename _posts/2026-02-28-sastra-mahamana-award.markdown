We are delighted to share that **Prof. R. N. Iyengar** has been conferred the prestigious **SASTRA-Mahamana Award for Excellence in Indian Knowledge Systems** by **SASTRA Deemed University** on **28-Feb-2026**.

The award recognizes his outstanding contributions in integrating traditional Indian knowledge with modern research. Prof. Iyengar's work has been a pioneering bridge between classical textual traditions and contemporary scientific inquiry, and this honor is a fitting acknowledgement of his lifelong scholarship.
